Cool information about Egyptian Arabic

  1. Also known as Masri, this dialect of Arabic is spoken by over 100 million people.
  2. This dialect is spoken mainly in areas of:
    • Cairo
    • Alexandria
    • Port Said
    • And many other areas of the Nile Delta.
  3. While this has long since been the dominant language of the region, it has also been influenced by older languages such as Ancient Egyptian.
  4. This dialect of the Araibic language is well understood across the ME/NA region because of Egypts successful entertainment industry.
